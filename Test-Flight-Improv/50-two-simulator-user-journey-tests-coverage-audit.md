## Rubric

- `unit`: deterministic tests under `test/` outside `*/integration/`, `test/integration/`, and `integration_test/`
- `integration`: tests under `test/**/integration/`, `test/integration/`, or non-smoke flows under `integration_test/`
- `fake_network`: fake/chaos/mock transport coverage, including dedicated fake harness flows
- `smoke`: files or scripts with `smoke` in the name, plus broad smoke/e2e confidence flows
- `sufficient`: direct evidence for the scenario’s core contract
- `partial`: adjacent or split coverage, but not the full journey
- `missing`: no meaningful direct automated evidence found
