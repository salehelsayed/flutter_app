How Redemption Works
Two methods — both use anonymous blind tokens. The business verifies the deal, never the user's identity.
📱 Show to Shop
User shows QR, cashier scans with their phone
1
Open your deal in the Deals tab
2
Show QR code on your screen to the cashier
3
Cashier scans with their phone via business.mknoun.xyz
4
Deal verified ✓ — discount applied to your order
5
+€0.80 credited to your wallet automatically
📷 Scan at Shop
User scans shop's QR sticker, gets a code
1
Spot the mknoon QR sticker near the register
2
Scan it with your mknoon app
3
Tap "Redeem" — get a 4-digit code
4
Tell the cashier your code (e.g. "7294")
5
Cashier verifies → +€0.80 credited
Show to Shop Scan at Shop
Shop effort Needs phone to scan Just a printed QR sticker
User effort Open app, show screen Scan + say a code
Speed ~5 seconds ~10 seconds
Best for Chains, cafés with tablets Small shops, market stalls
🔒 Both methods use anonymous blind tokens. The business sees "valid deal code" — never who you are.
